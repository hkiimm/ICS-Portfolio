FUTURE PROJECTS



APPLICATION

This application would store and keep track of all the clothing items you own. Basically it would be a virtual closet. It could keep track of any new purchases that you should start wearing, or it would tell you to donate any clothes that you haven't worn over the last year. You would be able to customize features such as how minimalistic you want to keep your closet, and what types of items you like to purchase. If you need to attend an event, and you need a certain item, it would browse stores that sell the article that you need. If you liked certain brands, such as nike, it would alert you whenever this brand is having a sale, or the item you're looking for is one sale. This would help you budget and save money. This application would be for people who like to buy clothes, hoard clothes (like me), or are just very disorganized. It would help you choose outfits in the morning or for special ocassions, so you don't have to stand in front of your closet thinking, "Why do I have no clothes?" This application would be helpful to keep people organized.
