FINAL PROJECT



ICS4U0 - ISP



This is a program that I wrote for my ICS4U0 final project. It is a game where the player learns about peer pressure and how to 'Deal With It'. There are three different levels where you learn, apply the knowledge, and deal with the peer pressure on your own. It is an interactive story game following the main character, you. You are discovering the dangers of peer pressure and learning how to deal with it. You must be able to choose how to deal with peer pressure accordingly in this decision based game. Throughout the game, you learn how to stand up to the mean bullies in your school, who try to peer pressure you. You also learn to be accepting of yourself and love who you are.
